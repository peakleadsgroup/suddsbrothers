About Sudds Brothers — Local Pressure Washing Experts

Passionate about helping homeowners keep their properties clean, healthy, and protected.

Meet Elliot Stanford

Elliot Stanford founded Sudds Brothers Pressure Washing with a straightforward mission: help homeowners across coastal North Carolina protect their biggest investment with professional exterior cleaning they can trust.

After seeing how mold, mildew, and algae buildup can quietly damage siding, roofs, and hard surfaces over time, Elliot Stanford set out to offer a better way — combining professional-grade equipment with natural cleaning solutions that are safe for your home, yard, family, and pets.

What started as a commitment to doing right by neighbors in Wilmington has grown into a full-service pressure washing company serving communities across the greater coastal NC region. Every job is approached with the same care and attention to detail Elliot Stanford would expect at his own home.

Healthy Homes Start with a Clean Exterior

Your home's exterior does more than look good — it protects everything inside. Organic growth like mold, mildew, and algae can spread across siding, roofs, and walkways, creating an environment that affects curb appeal and the long-term health of your property.

At Sudds Brothers, we're passionate about helping homeowners stay ahead of those problems. Regular professional cleaning removes harmful buildup before it causes costly damage, keeping your home looking its best year after year.

Natural Cleaning Solutions You Can Feel Good About

We believe effective cleaning shouldn't come at the expense of your family's safety or the environment around your home. That's why Sudds Brothers uses natural, biodegradable cleaning solutions designed to:

  • Eliminate mold, mildew, algae, and dirt without harsh chemicals
  • Protect your landscaping, lawn, and outdoor living spaces
  • Keep your home and yard safe for children, pets, and guests
  • Deliver lasting results that help prevent regrowth

Whether we're soft washing your siding or power washing your driveway, you can trust that our methods are tough on grime and gentle on what matters most.
